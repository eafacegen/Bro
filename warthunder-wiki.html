War Thunder

War Thunder is a free-to-play vehicular combat multiplayer video game developed and published by Gaijin Entertainment. The game is based around combined arms battles on air, land, and sea, featuring vehicles from the Spanish Civil War to the present day.

Overview

War Thunder offers a realistic and immersive combat experience with a vast array of military vehicles from different nations. The game features detailed damage models, realistic physics, and authentic vehicle characteristics.
